The Simple 5-Step PlantPal Experience

STEP ONE

Launch & Welcome

Open our app and be greeted with a soothing UI designed for nature lovers. The home screen offers 2 basic options to get started: Identify a Plant or View Saved Plants.

STEP TWO

Dashboard

Use your phone's camera to take a photo of your plant, or seamlessly import an image from your gallery. Our system is optimized for high-quality input.

STEP THREE

Capture or Import

Tap any Button and get the image into the box

STEP FOUR

Analyze & Identify

Tap the "Analyze Plant button. This sends the image to the powerful PlantNet API, which uses AI to provide the most likely species and confidence scores.

STEP FIVE

Detailed Data Preview and Timely Care & Conclusion

View the full identification results, including scientific names and your final step is getting personalized care instructions. PlantPal makes your job easier by reminding you to water and nourish your plants, ensuring their health and growth.
